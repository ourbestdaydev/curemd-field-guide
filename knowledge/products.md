# CureMD — Products & Platform

> Compiled mid-2026 by fetching the CureMD product/platform pages. Substance only — marketing fluff and sales calls-to-action removed. Page-by-page, each entry lists the source URL and any documentation links found.

## All-in-One Platform — https://www.curemd.com/all-in-one.asp
- Integrated cloud suite combining Electronic Health Records, Practice Management, and medical billing.
- Electronic Health Records: automated documentation ("Auto Note"), template library and custom forms, clinical decision support knowledge base, document imaging, electronic consents, flow sheets, body charts.
- Practice Management: enterprise scheduling with real-time eligibility verification, claim scrubbing, denial management, electronic referrals, clearinghouse integration, business intelligence reporting, task management, dashboards.
- Medical billing services: full revenue cycle management with dedicated teams, claims follow-up, appeals, accounts-receivable aging, productivity analytics, enrollment assistance, secondary billing.
- Patient portal: appointment requests, prescription refills, lab/radiology results, clinical summaries, electronic messaging, alerts.
- Certified for Meaningful Use Stage 3, the Medicare Access and Children's Health Insurance Program Reauthorization Act, and the Merit-based Incentive Payment System; supports Chronic Care Management and Remote Patient Monitoring.

**Documentation links:** /pdf/curemd-new.pdf (brochure); /resource-center.asp; https://blog.curemd.com

## Electronic Health Records — https://www.curemd.com/ehr.asp
- Cloud-based clinical documentation; Office of the National Coordinator 2015 Edition certified; Meaningful Use Stages 2 and 3 compliant; built-in ICD-10 coding.
- Electronic prescribing: 40,000+ pharmacies; drug knowledge base, medication reconciliation, pharmacy history, age/weight-based dosing, controlled-substance e-prescribing, real-time eligibility/formulary checks, drug-drug/drug-allergy alerts.
- Laboratory integration: electronic ordering and results from multiple labs, result trending/comparison, abnormal-value alerts.
- Interoperability: connects with health information exchanges, payers, radiology/imaging (DICOM), hospital networks, referring providers, cancer registries, syndromic surveillance, immunization registries, specialty registries.
- "Avalon" iPad/iPhone app: appointments, clinical summaries, note creation, charge collection, document management, eligibility verification.
- 30+ specialty-specific EHR variants.

**Documentation links:** /pdf/curemd.pdf; /pdf/leap.pdf (patient engagement); /pdf/curemd-for-public-health.pdf; Avalon iOS app; CureMD Kiosk iOS app

## Practice Management — https://www.curemd.com/practice-management.asp
- Cloud enterprise practice management connecting clinical, operational, and financial workflows.
- Multi-provider/multi-location scheduling with eligibility checks; billing with automated charge capture, clean-claim checking, electronic submission, payment posting; rules-based denial management; collections with check-in balance display and automated email/text reminders.
- Inventory management (medications, vaccines, durable medical equipment) with barcode scanning, multi-location, lot/expiration tracking; point of sale with integrated credit-card processing and payment plans; document management; secure messaging; KPI dashboards.
- Technical: web-based point-and-click, built-in clearinghouse for electronic data interchange, role-based access control with audit trails, SSL encryption, digital signatures. HIPAA and Meaningful Use Stage 3 certified.

**Documentation links:** /pdf/curemd.pdf; /resource-center.asp

## Patient Portal — "Leap Health" — https://www.curemd.com/patient-portal.asp
- The patient portal is branded **Leap Health** (separate iOS and Android apps).
- Functions for patients and guardians: schedule/reschedule/cancel appointments; locate providers; a "longitudinal health record" (complete health-history timeline) and tools to understand test results; prescription refill requests with adherence reminders; secure messaging with care teams plus a 24/7 AI assistant; view/pay bills online and in-visit digital co-pay at kiosks; digital check-in/intake, demographic updates, insurance verification; telehealth; record sharing with providers/family; health education; wearable-device integration.
- Note: guardian access is implied ("patients and guardians") but the page does not detail parent/child proxy mechanics — confirm with CureMD.

**Documentation links:** /pdf/leap.pdf (brochure); Leap iOS app; Leap Android app (com.curemd.leap); CureMD Kiosk iOS app

## Avalon — Mobile EHR — https://www.curemd.com/avalon.asp
- CureMD's mobile clinician app (iOS + Android), synchronized with the desktop EHR.
- Real-time documentation (notes, orders, labs, follow-ups); patient management; integrated e-prescribing; mobile scheduling; on-demand eligibility verification; image capture; voice input via Siri dictation.

**Documentation links:** Apple App Store (avalon-emr/id1095605631); Google Play (com.curemd.avalon)

## SMART Cloud EMR — https://www.curemd.com/emr_smart_cloud.asp
- Hosted software-as-a-service: no hardware/setup/maintenance cost. Bundle includes hosting, implementation, training, support, backup, security, updates, maintenance, data migration, electronic fax, credit-card processing, text messaging, patient statements, transcription, consulting.
- Connects with "thousands of pharmacies, labs, hospitals, radiology, payers, patients and other providers."

**Documentation links:** /whitepaper.asp; /pdf/curemd.pdf; /resource-center.asp

## Revenue Cycle Management / Medical Billing — https://www.curemd.com/rcm/index.html
- Outsourced revenue cycle management: denial management/appeals, payer-contract evaluation, claim clearance/submission, collections reorganization. Stated outcomes: higher clean-claims rate, faster payments, better receivables visibility.

**Documentation links:** "6 Ways to Improve Revenue Cycle Management" whitepaper (form-gated).

## Laboratory Integration — "CureLINK" — https://www.curemd.com/services_lab.asp
- **CureLINK** is CureMD's proprietary **HL7-compliant** lab interface engine: bi-directional electronic orders/results, configurable.
- Electronic order placement with automatic CPT coding; real-time result integration; pending/received review interface; patient-portal result access.
- Off-the-shelf labs: Quest Diagnostics, LabCorp, SunRise, Bio Reference, Antek. Custom device support: ECG/EKG, X-Ray, MRI, dialysis, ultrasound, vital-signs monitors.

**Documentation links:** none lab-specific (/pdf/curemd.pdf only).

## Data Migration — https://www.curemd.com/datamigration.asp
- Migrates/consolidates legacy data "regardless of the format and system," including medical-device outputs.
- Nine-step methodology: infrastructure assessment → migration planning → legacy access/extraction → data profiling → cleansing → transformation → quality monitoring → source backups → migration audit-trail documentation.
- No published list of supported source EHR platforms or validation metrics.

**Documentation links:** none.

## Discrete Reportable Transcription (DRT) — https://www.curemd.com/drt.asp
- Transcription service converting dictation into structured clinical notes that auto-populate the EMR (this is **not** disaster recovery). Provider dictates via toll-free number → transcribed overnight into pre-approved SOAP templates → provider reviews/approves → feeds EMR and billing. Three-tier quality control.

**Documentation links:** none DRT-specific.

- Data migration is a structured nine-step service claiming format-agnostic import (no published source-system list).
